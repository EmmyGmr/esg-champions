# Contributor Agreements

## Overview

This document outlines the agreements that contributors to the ESG Champions Platform must accept. By registering and contributing to the platform, you agree to these terms.

---

## 1. Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

### Purpose
The Contributor License Agreement ensures that contributions made to the ESG Champions Platform can be used, shared, and built upon by the community and STIF.

### Terms

By accepting this CLA, you agree that:

1. **Grant of Rights**: You grant STIF (Sustainability Technology and Innovation Forum) a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, display, perform, sublicense, and distribute your contributions.

2. **Original Work**: You represent that each contribution is your original work or you have the right to submit it.

3. **No Warranty**: You provide your contributions "as is" without warranty of any kind.

4. **Right to Grant License**: You have the legal right to grant the above license.

5. **Notification of Changes**: You agree to notify STIF if you become aware that any of your representations are inaccurate.

### What This Covers

- Reviews and ratings submitted for ESG indicators
- Comments and discussions
- Votes and community feedback
- Profile information you choose to make public
- Any other content you submit to the platform

### What This Does NOT Cover

- Your personal data (covered by Privacy Policy)
- Confidential information you've marked as such
- Content from external sources you reference

---

## 2. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) - Optional

### Purpose
The optional NDA provides additional protection for contributors who may share sensitive information during their participation.

### Terms

By accepting this NDA, you agree that:

1. **Confidential Information**: You will treat as confidential any non-public information shared by STIF or other platform members that is marked as confidential.

2. **Use Restrictions**: You will not use confidential information for any purpose other than your participation in the ESG Champions Platform.

3. **Non-Disclosure**: You will not disclose confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.

4. **Duration**: This obligation continues for 2 years after your last interaction with the confidential information.

5. **Exclusions**: This does not apply to information that:
   - Was already known to you
   - Becomes publicly available
   - Is independently developed by you
   - Is required to be disclosed by law

### Scope

The NDA applies to:
- Pre-release features or information
- Private discussions with STIF team
- Research data shared with you
- Strategic planning information

---

## 3. Code of Conduct

All contributors agree to:

### Be Respectful
- Treat all community members with respect
- Provide constructive feedback
- Avoid personal attacks or harassment

### Be Honest
- Submit accurate reviews based on expertise
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Do not manipulate ratings or votes

### Be Professional
- Maintain confidentiality where required
- Represent your credentials accurately
- Follow platform guidelines

### Be Collaborative
- Share knowledge to benefit the community
- Help new members
- Contribute to discussions constructively

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

### Your Content
You retain ownership of your original contributions while granting STIF the license described in the CLA.

### Platform Content
STIF and the ESG Champions Platform retain all rights to:
- Platform design and code
- ESG panel and indicator frameworks
- Aggregate data and analytics
- Brand and trademarks

### Third-Party Content
When referencing external sources:
- Cite properly
- Respect copyright
- Do not plagiarize

---

## 5. Attribution

STIF may attribute your contributions in the following ways:

- Display your name with your reviews (using your profile name)
- Include your contributions in reports and publications
- Reference your expertise in marketing materials (with consent)

You may request to be attributed anonymously for specific contributions.

---

## 6. Termination

### Your Rights
You may:
- Request deletion of your account and personal data
- Withdraw from the platform at any time
- Request anonymization of past contributions

### STIF's Rights
STIF may:
- Suspend accounts for violations
- Remove content that violates guidelines
- Modify these agreements with notice

## 7. Amendments

STIF may update these agreements periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
